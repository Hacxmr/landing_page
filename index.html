GRACE HOPPER CELEBRATION
SCHOLAR

The Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC) Scholarship is awarded to women in technology, providing them with the opportunity to attend the annual Grace Hopper Celebration, the world's largest gathering of women technologists. The scholarship covers conference registration, travel, and accommodation expenses. It aims to support and inspire the next generation of women in computing by offering access to career development workshops, networking opportunities, and exposure to leading tech companies.
